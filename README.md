# Technical Walkthrough 
Our team aimed to simplify the process of cooking for young individuals who struggle with learning new recipes and preparing meals quickly. Rather than relying on YouTube videos or cookbooks, users can input the ingredients they have available in their pantry/fridge and instantly discover recipes they can make. This personalized approach eliminates the limitations of predefined directories, ensuring accessibility for people of all cultural backgrounds.
# Key Features
- Ingredient-Based Search: Users can input the ingredients they have, and the app suggests recipes accordingly.
- Cultural Inclusivity: Our database includes recipes from various cultures, ensuring a diverse selection.
- Personalization: Users can save favorite recipes and adjust preferences for future recommendations.
- Simplified UI: Easy-to-use interface for a seamless user experience, particularly designed for young users.
